# Spaceship Constraint: Douglas Adams Only

When generating the spaceship list, all entries must come exclusively from Douglas Adams' written works. No other authors are permitted.

## In-scope works

- *The Hitchhiker's Guide to the Galaxy* (and the full five-book trilogy)
- *Starship Titanic* (novel by Terry Jones, based on Adams' game/concept — include only if Adams originated it)
- Any other novel, story, or essay written or co-written by Douglas Adams

## Out of scope

Any spaceship from a non-Adams book, even if it appeared in an Adams-adjacent adaptation (film, TV, game).

Draw from your knowledge of Adams' complete works to identify ships. If 5 distinct ships cannot be sourced from Adams' works, surface fewer entries rather than drawing from other authors.
